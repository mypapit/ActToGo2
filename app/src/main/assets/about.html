AcToGo application is constructed by using Android Studio. The Actuary on the Go prototype application can be installed in electronic devices especially i smartphones with Android operating system. The information provided within the Android application relates to Principles of Risk Management and Insurance course. One of the courses taken by actuarial and mathematical students in Universiti Teknologi MARA, Malaysia. The tools that have been set up in the application are study notes in PDF format, dictionary where users can key in the words or terminology they wish to search for and the application will give the definition, videos that pertain to insurance and risk, and other supported materials. The purpose of this application is namely to help students and lecturers alike to fully utilize their electronic device to act as a textbook aid which they can bring and access anywhere hence the name "Actuary on the Go"

Benefits

ActToGo is one of the applications that is useful particularly for actuarial students as well as other students who take actuary's course as an elective course. It provides an alternative method and added materials in searching for information about insurance and risk management just by installing the application in their smarthphone. Since it is hard to find interactive platform relating to actuarial science, this application has huge potential in assisting the actuarial academicians or researchers to find actuarial terms, definitions and formulas on the applications. It also helps the actuarial students to understand basic concept and principles of risk management and insurance which may be one of the methods for them to study or prepare for their final examination easily.
